About X:\Cell Financial Modelling

We believe that for today’s graduates, a knowledge of financial modelling is not just important, but critical for those who want to further their careers in financial services and industry.

In our experience, university and internships do not adequately prepare graduates for taking on complex modelling challenges in a real world-setting.

We have a passion for Financial Modelling and have designed a course that will address this learning gap. In just two days, students will learn how to build a Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Financial Model from scratch, including modelling fundamentals and best practice, shortcuts and time saving tips and real-world advice and experience.
